Steak

steak
  • rib eye steak
  • black pepper
  • sea salt
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • cayenne pepper
  • ground turmeric
  • ground coriander
  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Stir the salt, paprika, black pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, coriander, and turmeric together in a small bowl; set aside.
  3. Rub the steaks on all sides with the seasoning mixture.
  4. Cook on the preheated grill to your desired degree of doneness, 3 to 3 1/2 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  5. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 130 degrees F (54 degrees C).
